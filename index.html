What Makes A Good Visualization?

Good Visualization

This Visualization struck me as a good example because it covered the main five topics: Truthful, Function, Beautiful, Insightful, and Enlightening. The visualization depicts accuate information with plenty data and research to prove its point. Functionality is key to a good design, and the purpose of the visualization informs the audience of food waste sufficiently. Eye-Catching is what draws the intended audience in to observe the visualization, and more importantly incorporate the information. Furthermore, after reading the information then the audience is informed about food waste, or the data supporting it. Lastly, the visualization creates a feeling of change or movement to take action and fix the problem presented.
